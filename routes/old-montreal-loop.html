Safety Notes

Cobblestones Challenges

  • Bumpy and slippery when wet. Go slow
  • Your bike will rattle. That's normal
  • Watch for gaps between stones (wheels can catch)

Pedestrian Heavy

  • Tourists everywhere. Unpredictable movement
  • Ring your bell constantly. Be ultra-defensive
  • Walk your bike on the most crowded segments (especially Rue Saint-Paul in summer)

Car Traffic (Interior Streets)

  • Saint-Jacques and interior streets have car traffic
  • Watch for turning cars
  • Stay visible and predictable
