Legendary Poutine

Frite Alors

Iconic poutine on St-Denis. The institution. The classic. Post-ride refuel. Cheap, delicious, authentically Montréal. Where cyclists have fueled up for decades.

The Iconic Poutine Stop

Frite Alors is not fancy. It's not trying to be anything other than what it is: a poutine counter serving the best fries and gravy in the city. Located on St-Denis in the Latin Quarter, it's been an institution for generations. For cyclists, it's the perfect post-ride refuel stop.

This is Montréal poutine at its purest: crispy fries, hot gravy, real cheese curds. No frills. No fusion versions. Just the classic. And it's delicious.

The Menu

Classic Poutine

  • Frites Seules (Fries Only)
    $4–5. Perfect golden fries. Crispy outside, soft inside. Worth getting on its own.
  • Poutine Régulière (Classic)
    $6–7. Fries + gravy + cheese curds. The iconic. The standard. The one to get.
  • Poutine Spéciale
    $8–10. Extra toppings (meat, mushrooms, etc.). Good if you're extra hungry.

Why This Poutine?

Fresh fries (cooked to order). Real cheese curds that squeak. Hot gravy that doesn't get soggy immediately. Classic proportions. No frozen pre-cooked fries. No powdered gravy. This is the real thing.

The Experience

Ordering

Line up. Order at the counter. Wait 5–10 minutes while they fry. Take your food and find a spot to eat (tables inside or outside).

Atmosphere

Casual. Young crowd. Students, cyclists, locals. French signage. No English menu (not designed for tourists). Authentic Montréal Latin Quarter energy.

Eating

Hot poutine is best eaten immediately. The fries cool fast. Grab napkins (lots of them). Eat standing or find a spot in the area. St-Denis has benches and seating areas nearby.

Why Cyclists Love It

Practical Information

Hours

Lunch → Late night (typical poutine counter hours)

Cost

$4–10 depending on what you order. Very affordable.

Bike Parking

St-Denis has abundant bike racks. Safe neighborhood. Popular student/cycling area.

Best Time

Post-ride (after a long ride, this hits different). Late afternoon or evening. Not recommended for sit-down destination — grab and go.
