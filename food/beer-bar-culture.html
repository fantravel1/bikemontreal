The Post-Ride Beer Culture

The Ritual

Finish your ride. Head to a beer bar with friends. Lock bikes outside. Sit. Order beer. Debrief the ride. Talk about routes, speed, observations. This is social cycling culture.

Group Rides Ending in Beer

Organize a group ride that ends at a beer bar. This is classic cyclist social. Meet at a starting point, ride together, arrive at the bar as a group, spend an evening together. Community building.

Solo Beer Stops

Finish a ride solo. Stop at a beer bar. Meet other cyclists. Become a regular. Build community through solo beer stops.

Bike Safety & Responsible Drinking

One Beer Rule

If biking home: one beer maximum. Cycling after drinking is dangerous. Plan accordingly: (1) Don't drink and bike home, (2) Take transit home, or (3) Uber home with your bike.

Helmet Storage

Leave your helmet with the bartender or at your table. Wearing it at the bar looks silly. Keep it secure.

Bike Parking

Always use a lock. Even in safe neighborhoods. Even for a quick beer. Thieves know bars have distracted customers.

Recovery After Drinking

Beer before food/water is a bad idea. Eat something before drinking. Hydrate. Don't bike home intoxicated.

Group Responsibility

If riding with friends, watch out for each other. Designate someone as the non-drinker or someone to call transit. Responsible group cycling culture is important.
