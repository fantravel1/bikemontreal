Where to Avoid

Dangerous Intersections

  • Saint-Laurent & Blvd Saint-Joseph: High car traffic, aggressive turning. Be ultra-careful
  • Rue Rachel & Ave du Parc: Tourist density + confused navigation. Slow down and signal

High-Stress Roads

  • Blvd Saint-Joseph (rush hours): Cars + buses, minimal protection
  • Rue Sherbrooke (western edge): Tourist area, car traffic, not bike-friendly

Confusing Segments

  • Rue Prince Arthur (east of Saint-Laurent): Pedestrian zone. Walk your bike or find an alternate route
  • Park transitions near Mount Royal: Watch for pedestrian traffic and unpredictable foot flow

Safety & Theft Reality

Where to Lock
