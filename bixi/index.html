What is BIXI?

BIXI (Bike Express) is Montr√©al's public bike-sharing system. Over 10,000 bikes at 800+ stations across the city. Pick up at any station, ride to your destination, dock at the nearest station. No membership required. Pay per ride or get a pass.

BIXI runs April through November. During winter, regular BIXI shuts down (but the REV Saint-Denis path stays cleared for year-round riders).

Quick Start: How to Use BIXI

1. Download the App

Download BIXI from App Store or Google Play. Create an account. Add a payment method (credit card).

2. Find a Bike

Open the app. See nearby stations and available bikes. Walk to the closest station with available bikes.

3. Unlock

At the station, scan the QR code on a bike or enter the bike number. Click "Unlock." The bike is yours.

4. Ride

Ride to your destination. Use protected paths when possible. Follow traffic rules.

5. Dock

Return to any BIXI station. Insert the bike into an available dock. You'll hear a click when it's locked. You're done.

Pro Tip: Keep Rides Under 30 Minutes

BIXI's pricing model rewards short trips. Keep rides under 30 minutes to avoid overage charges ($0.10/min after 30 min). Plan your route with docking stations in mind.

E-Bikes & Electric

BIXI also offers e-bikes at select stations. Higher price ($0.30/min vs $0.10/min), but perfect for longer distances, hills (Mount Royal!), or if you're tired.
