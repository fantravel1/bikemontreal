Night Riding Etiquette

Be Visible

Drivers don't expect cyclists at night. Lights and reflective gear are critical. Don't assume they see you.

Be Quiet

Night people are trying to sleep. Don't yell. Don't accelerate loudly. Keep noise minimal.

Be Predictable

No sudden moves. Signal turns clearly. Ride straight lines. Drivers need to anticipate your movement.

Be Respectful

You're biking through neighborhoods where people are out at night. Respect their space. Don't invade private areas.
